Characters

There are 49 playable characters in Broforce. This page will cover 3 of those characters.


Rambro

The first character

Rambro is the first character in the game and byfar the most simple.

Main attack Special Attack Ripped off from
Machine Gun Grenade Rambo

Mr.Anderbro

The best character

Mr.Anderbro is a character who is easy to use but incredibly powerful.

Main attack Special Attack Ripped off from
Meele Bullet Shield Matrix

Indiana Brones

The worst character

Indiana Brones is a character who has very little moves and just sucks.

Main attack Special Attack Ripped off from
Whip Revolver Indiana Jones
